Why is my car air conditioning not blowing cold?

The most common cause is a refrigerant leak — air con systems don't "use up" gas, so if it's low, it's escaping somewhere. Other causes include a failing compressor, a blocked condenser, or an electrical fault in the system. We leak-test properly before re-gassing, so you're fixing the actual problem rather than topping it up every few months.

What are the signs my alternator is failing?

Common signs include a dimming or flickering dashboard, a battery warning light, slow power windows, or the car struggling to start. Left unaddressed, a failing alternator will eventually drain the battery completely and leave you stranded — worth getting checked as soon as you notice symptoms.

Do you come to my house or work, or do I need to bring my car in?

We're a fully mobile service — we come to you, wherever the vehicle is: home, work, or a breakdown location. There's no workshop to drive to.

How much does a mobile auto electrician call-out cost?

Standard callouts are quoted per job once we know the vehicle and the issue. Emergency or same-day breakdown callouts attract an additional fee — call us and we'll confirm pricing on the spot.

Can you fit a dual battery system for touring or camping?

Yes — we design and install dual battery setups sized to what you actually run (fridges, lighting, camping gear), including DC-DC chargers and solar integration where needed.

My dashboard warning light is on but the car still drives fine — is it urgent?

It depends on the light, but it's worth having scanned sooner rather than later. Some warning lights flag issues that are fine to drive on short-term; others indicate a fault that gets more expensive to fix the longer it's ignored. We can run a full diagnostic scan to tell you exactly what it means.

Do you service caravans and trailers as well as cars?

Yes — we fit and repair electric brake controllers, trailer wiring and plugs, and general towing electrics for caravans and trailers, in addition to standard vehicle work.

What areas of the Sunshine Coast do you cover?

We're based in Little Mountain and cover the Sunshine Coast for standard bookings, with emergency callouts available up to 60km away. Call or send through your location and we'll confirm.
