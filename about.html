OUR STORY

We come to you —
home, work or breakdown.

Rockets Auto Electrics + Air Conditioning started with a simple idea: auto electrical work shouldn't mean a day off work waiting at a workshop. We bring the van, the scan tools and the parts to wherever your vehicle actually is — your driveway, your workplace car park, or the side of the road if it's broken down.

We're a genuinely local, family run operation across the Sunshine Coast. That means the person who answers the phone is the same person who shows up to do the job — no call centres, no subcontractors, no surprises.

Using the latest scan tools and quality aftermarket parts, we handle everything from alternator and starter repairs through to full air conditioning servicing, diagnostics, towing electrics and vehicle accessory fit-outs.
