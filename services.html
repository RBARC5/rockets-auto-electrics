CHARGING SYSTEM

Alternators & Starters

If your car's struggling to start, or the battery light's come on while you're driving, it's usually your alternator or starter motor. We diagnose the fault on the spot, and carry common replacement parts on the van so most jobs are fixed in a single visit — no towing to a workshop required.

  • Alternator testing, repair and replacement
  • Starter motor diagnosis and replacement
  • Battery and charging system health checks
  • On-site fitting — most jobs done in one visit
CLIMATE SYSTEM

Air Conditioning

Warm air blowing when it should be cold usually means a refrigerant leak or a failing component. We leak-test properly rather than just re-gassing and hoping, so you're not paying for the same fix twice.

  • Leak testing and diagnosis
  • Re-gas and full system service
  • Compressor, condenser and evaporator repairs
  • Pre-summer air con health checks
FAULT FINDING

Electrical Diagnosis

Dash warning lights, intermittent faults and gremlins that only show up sometimes — these need proper scan tools, not guesswork. We use dealer-level diagnostic equipment to find the actual fault, first time.

  • Warning light and fault code diagnosis
  • Intermittent electrical fault tracing
  • Wiring repairs and rewiring
  • Pre-purchase electrical inspections
TOWING SYSTEM

Electric Brake Controllers

Towing a trailer or caravan? We supply and fit electric brake controllers, wired in properly to your vehicle and tested under load — not just plugged in and hoped for the best.

  • Brake controller supply and installation
  • Trailer plug and wiring fit-out
  • Testing under towing load
  • Caravan and trailer electrical checks
POWER SYSTEM

Dual Battery & Cruise Control

Heading off-grid or just want more power on board? We fit dual battery systems sized to what you actually run — fridges, lighting, camping gear — plus cruise control installation for long-haul comfort.

  • Dual battery system design and installation
  • DC-DC chargers and solar integration
  • Cruise control installation
  • Touring and 4WD electrical setups
FIT-OUT

Audio, Lighting & Accessories

From a stereo upgrade to a full lighting and camera fit-out, we install it properly — wired in, fused correctly, and finished so it looks factory, not aftermarket.

  • Stereo and speaker upgrades
  • Driving lights and light bars
  • Reverse cameras and parking sensors
  • Immobiliser fitting
